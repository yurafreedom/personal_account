Reward Program

Reward Points

We have designed the most sophisticated reward program to offer to our community members irresistible and unbeatable benefits.

As long as you are tuned with us, your account will accumulate points.

If you are on social media or you interact with your family and friends, Mantra Tourus will reward you.

How this works?

• When your register as community member of Mantra Torus, you will receive a welcome bonus.

• When you refer any of your friends or family member and he/she registers as community member with Mantra Tourus, you will receive a referral bonus.

• Any time you like a post on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Quora and YouTube, you are rewarded for each like.

• Any time you share a post on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Quora and YouTube, you are rewarded for each share.

• Any time your registered referral likes a post on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Quora and YouTube, the referral is rewarded for each like and you are rewarded as well for each like.

• Any time your registered referral shares a post on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Quora and YouTube, the referral is rewarded for each share and you are rewarded as well for each share.

• Being a valuable community member, you will receive a birth gift on your birthday date.

And many other benefits and surprises are there to come.

Stay tunes and get the benefits designed only for you.
